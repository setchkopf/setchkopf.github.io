Accomodation

Accommodation in Bath is expensive and gets booked up early (we’re sorry!). We hope we’re getting married late enough in the day that some of you will be able to choose to come for the day but here are our best suggestions if you do need a place to stay:

  • There is a Travelodge in Beckington, 13 miles from our reception venue which has rooms available from £68.

  • Bristol South Premier Inn has rooms available from £62 and is 9.5 miles from Timsbury. Bristol accommodation is generally better value than Bath, as is Keynsham (7 miles).

  • The University of Bath has accommodation (single and double rooms) available in the summer holidays from £50 on their campus or in the city centre.

If accommodation costs are a real problem please talk to us – we might know someone with a spare room!
